About:

Who are we?

Visual Technology is a corporation that focuses on creating improved technologies for glasses.
Our main goal is to provide our customers with smarter solutions, that makes it easier for them to fulfill their daily activities and tasks.

What do we do?

We’re waging war on the standardized eyewear market with our unique product lines designed to aid both people with colorblindness as well as people with reading difficulties.
We combat reading difficulties by using smart technology that read words aloud which enhances comprehension.
We’ve securely triumphed over colorblindness by adapting and improving our own custom glasses with enhanced color contrast.

What is our mission?

Visual Technology’s mission is to decrease the struggles of those who are visually impaired.
Our team is working towards a bright future where one pair of glasses satisfy all customer needs.

Our upcoming product

Our next project, IC glasses 1.0, is set to revolutionize what humans expect out of everyday eyewear.
We’ve embarked on a quest to fuse our two product lines together into one all-purpose pair of glasses. This 2-in-1 special combination aims to diminish people’s glasses collection into one all-encompassing pair.
