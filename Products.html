The glasses are constructed to contain a various assortment of different technologies. These technologies work cohesively to create a piece of eyewear with immense accessibility.

Eye-tracking


Eye-tracking uses infrared light rays which reflects upon the eye and calculates the data from its movement patterns. This technology serves as a foundational element to implement other functionality.

Camera functions and focus:


To capture still images of what the +/- buttons is looking at, a camera situated at the front works in tandem with the eye-tracking. The glasses will then process the image-text and read it back to the user through speakers.


The focus button located on the left side functions like progressive glasses. It analyzes the scene through sensors which adjusts clarity. The buttons allows the user to digitally zoom in on objects.

Color filter:
